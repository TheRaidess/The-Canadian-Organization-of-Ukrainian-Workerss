Our Goals

The primary goals of COUW are:

  • Support the rights and well-being of Ukrainian workers in Canada.
  • Provide legal and advisory assistance on labor laws, workplace rights, and social protection.
  • Offer educational programs, including language training, professional development, and Canadian labor law education.
  • Promote the integration of Ukrainian workers into Canadian society through cultural and social initiatives.
  • Preserve and promote Ukrainian cultural heritage through events, festivals, and exhibitions.
