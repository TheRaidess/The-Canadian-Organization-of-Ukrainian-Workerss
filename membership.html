Membership

Who can become a member?

Anyone of Ukrainian descent or those actively supporting the rights of Ukrainian workers in Canada can join COUW.

Member Rights

  • Participate in General Meetings and vote on organizational matters.
  • Elect and be elected to governing bodies.
  • Receive information and participate in events organized by COUW.

Member Responsibilities

  • Adhere to the bylaws and regulations of the organization.
  • Regularly pay membership dues or provide other support.
  • Actively participate in achieving the organization's goals.
