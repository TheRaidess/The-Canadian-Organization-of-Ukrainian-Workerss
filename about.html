About Us

The Canadian Organization of Ukrainian Workers (COUW) is a public society registered under Canadian law. We are a non-union entity that supports and protects the rights and well-being of Ukrainian workers in Canada.

COUW focuses on the cultural, social, and economic integration of Ukrainian workers into Canadian society while preserving Ukrainian cultural identity.
